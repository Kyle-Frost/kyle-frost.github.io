About

Let me introduce myself.

My name is Kyle Frost, I have a strong interest in not only games, but how they work. I also enjoy the communities that surround games and have regularly taken part in eSport teams and events during my time at University. Development communities have been fun to be a part of, and my participation in game jams have allowed me to connect with others in a fun and helpful way.

Skills
